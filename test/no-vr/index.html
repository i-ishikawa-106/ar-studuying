BELISARIO, el pequeño gran héroe del cosmos

Sinopsis

"Belisario, the little big heroe of the cosmos" is a fulldome animated series for children and teenagers. This series tells about the adventures of a little astronaut mouse, who travels through time to register his name in Argentina's Astronautics history. Throughout his trips, he will witness the most relevant events and face unforeseen dangers that he will need to overcome to survive.
